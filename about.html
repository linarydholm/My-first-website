Once upon a time...

A little elf arrived to Santas workshop for another exciting day of gift wrapping. It was only one day until christmas, but this particular day something drastic was about to happen.

The little elf was excited, this was his first year at the workshop and he could only imagine what christmas would be like. He started his shift by wrapping a rubics cube, then an action figure. A few monents later a large toy came rolling on the conveyor. He was about to lift it down and little did he know that this toy was heavy and ended up falling over him.

He was knocked out and didn't woke up until he arrived at a hopsital.

At the hospital, the little elf started to notice how drained everybody seemed to be. He was talking to a lot of people and not only did the tragic stories about sickness and wounds burdened the people. They where also burned out from hosting christmas for family and friends.
